Motherlode is a mod for Minecraft that adds tons of new features. You could consider it an overhaul or an expansion mod.
It adds features such as new gems, tools, blocks, mobs among other cool features.

The plan for this mod is to unlock Minecraft's full potential; make it more engaging and fun to play.
The mod changes some fundamentals of the game, and you may have to relearn what you have learned, but ultimately it hopefully result in a much
better experience for you.
